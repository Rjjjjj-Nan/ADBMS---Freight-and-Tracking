How the Route is Optimized

  • Nearest Neighbor Algorithm: Starting from Manila (warehouse at 14.5995°N, 120.9842°E), the AI selects the closest unvisited destination, then builds the route sequentially.
  • Distance Calculation: Uses Haversine formula to calculate great-circle distance between Philippine coordinates.
  • Delivery Time Estimate: Based on 40 km/h average road speed + 1 hour processing per hub. Critical shipments get priority.
  • Cost Estimation: ₱100 base + ₱5/km, with multipliers: Regular (1.0x), Express (1.5x), Critical (2.0x)
  • Priority Coloring: Critical (orange) and Express (blue) shipments highlighted for visibility.
